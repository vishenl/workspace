Frequently Asked Questions

Is this right for beginners or only advanced practitioners?

+

Spiritual Mastery is designed for both beginners and advanced practitioners. If you're new to spirituality, you'll receive comprehensive foundational training in all practices. If you're experienced, you'll deepen your existing knowledge into true embodiment. The 7 Gates structure allows everyone to progress at their level while moving toward mastery together.

How much time per week is required?

+

Plan for 3-4 hours per week: Two 90-minute live sessions + one implementation workshop. All sessions are recorded, so if you can't attend live, you can watch on your schedule. Many students integrate daily practices (10-20 minutes) for accelerated results, but this is optional.

What if I can't attend live sessions?

+

Every session is recorded and available within 24 hours. You'll have 1 full year of access to replay any session. While attending live enhances the experience through Q&A and community connection, many students complete the program successfully using recordings on their own schedule.

Both. We integrate cutting-edge neuroscience, somatic psychology, and trauma research with ancient spiritual wisdom. Dr. Peter Levine's Somatic Experiencing is scientifically validated.
