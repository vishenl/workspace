V. VIDEO STRUCTURE (Priestley's Proven Formula)

The 3-Act Structure (Every Video):

Act 1: The Hook (First 15-30 seconds)

Pattern: Bold statement + Personal credibility + Promise Example: "Most people think meditation is about clearing your mind. After training 10 million students at Mindvalley, I can tell you that's the #1 reason people fail. Today I'm sharing the real approach that actually works."

Act 2: The Framework (Main Content)

  • Introduce ONE core framework
  • Break it into 3-5 clear steps
  • Use visual aids (screen/whiteboard)
  • Personal story illustrating each point
  • Practical, actionable examples
  • Time: 8-13 minutes

Act 3: The Close (Last 1-2 minutes)

  • Recap the framework in 30 seconds
  • ONE clear next step for viewer
  • Soft CTA to relevant Quest/resource
  • Ask for like/subscribe (briefly)
