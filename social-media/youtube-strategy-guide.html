XII. COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID

What Priestley Does Right (Model This):

  1. Consistency Over Perfection: He publishes on schedule, not when "perfect"
  2. Value Over Production: Message matters more than 4K cameras
  3. Frameworks Over Stories: Teach replicable systems, not just inspiration
  4. Business Mindset: Every video serves the business funnel
  5. Long-term Thinking: Building authority, not chasing viral moments

What To Avoid (Common Mistakes):

  • ‚ùå Over-production: Waiting for "perfect" setup instead of shipping
  • ‚ùå Rambling interviews: 60-minute podcasts instead of tight 15-min extractions
  • ‚ùå Chasing trends: Jumping on TikTok dances instead of staying in your lane
  • ‚ùå Sporadic publishing: Posting when you "feel inspired" instead of on schedule
  • ‚ùå No CTA: Creating value without directing viewers to next step
  • ‚ùå Broad targeting: Trying to please everyone instead of serving your specific audience
