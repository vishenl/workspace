## PART 5: SUCCESS METRICS & OPTIMIZATION

### Key Performance Indicators

**REGISTRATION METRICS:**

- Registration rate (clicks to registrations)
- Source tracking (email, social, ads, organic)
- Registration to show-up rate (target: 40-50%)

**WEBINAR ENGAGEMENT:**

- Average watch time (target: 60+ minutes of 90)
- Drop-off points (where people leave)
- Chat engagement (messages per minute)
- Poll participation rate

**CONVERSION METRICS:**

- Live conversion rate (enrolled during/immediately after webinar)
- Replay conversion rate (enrolled after watching replay)
- 24-hour conversion rate
- 48-hour conversion rate
- Overall conversion rate (target: 3-8% for high-ticket)

**REVENUE METRICS:**

- Average order value (base vs premium vs VIP)
- Total revenue per webinar
- Revenue per registrant
- Revenue per attendee

**TIER DISTRIBUTION:**

- % Base Voyage
- % Premium (+$250)
- % VIP (+$500)
- Goal: Sell all 8 VIP first, then Premium, then Base

### Optimization Strategy

**PRE-WEBINAR OPTIMIZATION:**

1. **A/B Test Email Subject Lines:**
   - Curiosity-driven: "The year-end ritual that changes everything"
   - Benefit-driven: "How to architect your extraordinary 2026"
   - Urgency-driven: "Last chance to transform your 2026 (Limited seats)"

2. **A/B Test Registration Page:**
   - Version A: Long-form copy with full benefits
   - Version B: Short-form with video preview
   - Measure conversion rate

3. **Optimize Show-Up Rate:**
   - Send 4 reminder emails (7 days, 3 days, 1 day, 1 hour)
   - SMS reminders for high-intent registrants
   - Calendar invites with automated reminders

**DURING-WEBINAR OPTIMIZATION:**

1. **Monitor Drop-Off:**
   - If drop-off happens at 20-min mark, problem phase is too long
   - If drop-off happens at 60-min mark, solution phase needs more engagement
   - Adjust pacing for next webinar

2. **Test Different Practices:**
   - Webinar A: Include 2 mini-practices
   - Webinar B: Include 1 longer practice
   - Measure engagement and conversion

3. **Test Price Reveal Timing:**
   - Version A: Reveal price at 80 minutes (standard)
   - Version B: Reveal price at 75 minutes (earlier)
   - Measure conversion and objections

**POST-WEBINAR OPTIMIZATION:**

1. **Email Sequence Testing:**
   - Test different email sequences (3-day vs 5-day vs 7-day)
   - Test emotional vs logical messaging
   - Test urgency timing (48-hour vs 72-hour window)

2. **Replay Strategy:**
   - Limited-time replay (48 hours only) vs Extended replay (7 days)
   - Measure replay views and conversion rates

3. **Retargeting Ads:**
   - Facebook/Instagram retargeting for registrants who didn't buy
   - Show social proof ads (testimonials, sold-out seats)

### Continuous Improvement

**AFTER EACH WEBINAR:**

- Conduct debrief with team
- Analyze all metrics
- Survey attendees (both buyers and non-buyers)
- Identify top objections that weren't addressed
- Refine script for next iteration

**QUESTIONS TO ASK:**

- Where did people drop off?
- Which stories resonated most?
- What objections kept coming up?
- What price tier sold best?
- What was the average time-to-purchase?

**ITERATE AND IMPROVE:**

- Version 1.0: First webinar (baseline)
- Version 1.1: Refined based on feedback (10% improvement)
- Version 1.2: Optimized based on data (20% improvement)
- Version 2.0: Major overhaul if needed (30%+ improvement)
