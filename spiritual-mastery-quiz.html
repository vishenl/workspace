Welcome to Your Spiritual Assessment

This comprehensive assessment measures three key dimensions of spiritual mastery using scientifically validated scales.

Well-Being (WHO-5)

Your day-to-day flourishing and emotional vitality over the past two weeks. A global standard for measuring psychological well-being.

Mystical/Awe Experiences (MEQ-30)

The depth and quality of peak spiritual states, awe, and mystical insights from your recent practices. Validated in consciousness research.

Calm Under Pressure (PSS-10)

Your stress resilience and ability to remain centered during life's challenges. Widely used in clinical and research settings.

Time commitment:

This assessment takes approximately 10-15 minutes to complete. Please find a quiet space where you can answer honestly and reflectively. Your responses will help you track your spiritual growth over time.
