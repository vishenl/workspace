‚ö° The 5 Golden Rules of Copywriting

Master these fundamentals before touching AI tools

Rule #1: Write for a 15-Year-Old Reading Level

Why it matters: Multiple studies prove that content matching a 15-year-old's reading level converts better across ALL audiences, regardless of education level.

How to Test Your Readability:

  1. Go to HemingwayApp.com

    This free tool analyzes your text readability

  2. Copy your website text

    Select all the copy from your homepage or landing page

  3. Paste it into Hemingway

    The tool will instantly analyze your content

  4. Check your grade level

    Aim for grade 5-8 (approximately 10-15 years old)

‚úÖ Real Example from the Masterclass:

When Henri tested her website for female founders, it came back as Grade 5 (approximately 10-11 years old). Perfect! This proves that even complex transformation work can be explained simply and clearly.

Rule #2: Have ONE Clear Call to Action (CTA)

The Problem: Most websites confuse visitors with multiple competing CTAs.

‚ùå Common Mistake - Too Many CTAs:

  • "Let's Book Clarity"
  • "Empower"
  • "Impact"
  • "Free Guide"
  • "Reignite Your Flame"

Result: Visitor confusion = No action taken

‚úÖ The Solution - ONE Primary CTA:

"Download Your Free Guide"

This button appears 2-3 times throughout the page. Everything else is secondary or removed.

Rule #3: It's Not About Making a Sale - It's About Getting Users to USE the Product

The Mindvalley $1M Case Study:

‚ùå Initial Approach (2005):

"Download your free audio and ebook" ‚Üí Pop-up ‚Üí Instant download

Result: 20% MORE emails, but 30% FEWER sales üìâ

Why? People weren't reading the ebook because we didn't explain why they needed it.

‚úÖ The Solution:

Create a dedicated landing page that:

  • Explains what's in the guide
  • Shows the benefits they'll receive
  • Demonstrates how to use it
  • Includes testimonials and social proof
  • Makes a compelling case for why they need it

Result: Sales recovered and exceeded previous levels ‚ú®

üéØ Key Insight: Sell Them on the Free Product

Even if something is free, you must convince people to consume it. The sale isn't done when they buy‚Äîit's done when they actively use your product.

Rule #4: All Buttons Must Have Consistent Messaging

The messaging on your buttons really matters. Small changes can make a big difference in conversions.

‚úÖ Examples of Effective Button Copy:

  • First Purchase: "Add to Cart"
  • Upsell After Purchase: "Add to My Order" (not "Add to Cart" again)
  • Free Download: "Send Me the Free Guide" (appears consistently)
  • Strategy Call: "Book Your Free Strategy Call"
‚ùå Confusing Button Copy Example:

  • "Get My Free Guide Now"
  • "Send Me the Free Guide"
  • "Take a Three Minute Quiz"
  • "Book Your Clarity Call"
  • "Click and Check"

Too many different messages = Confused visitors who don't take action

Rule #5: Get Your Audience to Raise Their Hand (Call Out)

When visitors come to your website, they should immediately know if it's for them.

‚úÖ Good Call Outs:

  • "A 10-Minute Guide for Female Founders, Coaches, and Changemakers Whose Fire Feels Dim"
  • "Attention International Medical Graduates and Future Physicians Worldwide"
  • "For Ambitious Entrepreneurs Who Want to Scale Without Burning Out"

üí° Why This Works:

When you're specific about who your product is for:

  • The right people feel seen and stay
  • The wrong people leave (which is good!)
  • You keep your email list concentrated
  • Higher open rates and better deliverability
  • Better conversion rates overall

Create a ChatGPT trained specifically on YOUR market and audience

Why You DON'T Need to Learn Traditional Copywriting

Learning world-class copywriting can take 5-10 years. Vishen spent 8 years studying it, attended 10+ conferences, and invested over $20,000 in courses. You don't have to do that anymore.

With AI, you only need to understand the fundamentals (the 5 Golden Rules), then let AI do the heavy lifting.

The 3-Step Process

  1. Create a Deep Research Prompt

    Ask ChatGPT to interview you about your market and create a research prompt tailored to your audience.

    Example Prompt: 
    I want you to give me a deep research prompt to create
a copywriting guide for a specific market.

My market is: [Describe your audience]

Ask me clarifying questions to understand my market better.
  2. Run Deep Research

    Take the research prompt and run it through ChatGPT's Deep Research mode. It will spend 20 minutes analyzing your market and creating a comprehensive copywriting guide (60-70 pages).

    ChatGPT Will Ask You Questions Like:

    • What country is your primary market?
    • What kind of businesses do your clients run?
    • What services/products do you offer?
    • What platforms do you use (Instagram, LinkedIn, email)?
    • Do you want to emulate any specific brands?
    • What's your founder story?
    • What feeling should your brand evoke?
  3. Create Your Custom GPT

    Upload the research guide to a custom GPT. Now you have a copywriting assistant that knows your market, your voice, and your audience.

    How to Use Your Custom GPT: 
    Help me analyze this copy and improve it.

Case Study #2: Manik - US Medical Residency Coaching

‚ùå Before: The Paradox of Choice Problem

  • Multiple products displayed prominently on homepage
  • Confusing button labels: "Click and Check"
  • "Book Free Strategy Call" CTA disappeared at bottom of page
  • Too many options causing decision paralysis
  • Weak call out - didn't clearly identify the target audience

Classic "Paradox of Choice" - like the jam study where offering 20 jams instead of 5 actually decreased sales.

‚úÖ Recommended Improvements:

  • Stronger Call Out: "Attention International Medical Graduates and Future Physicians Worldwide" (bigger, bolder)
  • ONE Primary CTA: Book Free Strategy Call (appears throughout page and at bottom)
  • Strategy Call Landing Page: Dedicated page explaining what happens on the call before calendar
  • Simplify Buttons: Remove confusing "Click and Check" messaging
  • Hide Secondary Products: Focus on the main conversion path first

The Strategy Call Landing Page Template

Don't send people directly to a calendar. Create a page that sells them on the call:

  • Headline: "Book Your Free Strategy Call"
  • What to Expect: Clear breakdown of the 30-minute call
  • What You'll Get:
    • Clarity on your pathway to US residency
    • Custom learning path based on your background
    • Timeline and deadlines for your specialty
    • Rotation recommendations
  • Meet Your Advisor: Photo, credentials, experience
  • Call Details: "30 minutes ‚Ä¢ Free ‚Ä¢ No obligation"
  • Social Proof: "We've guided 10,000+ international medical graduates"

This approach increases both booking rate AND show-up rate dramatically!

Key Insight: The Paradox of Choice

Robert Cialdini's famous jam study: A supermarket displayed 5 types of jam and had good sales. They increased to 20 varieties, and sales DROPPED.

üí° The Lesson for Your Website:

More options = Less conversions. Give visitors ONE clear path to follow, not multiple choices that create decision paralysis.

üöÄ Your 30-Minute Action Plan

Transform your website copy today with this step-by-step guide

Phase 1: Quick Audit (10 Minutes)

  1. Test Readability

    Go to HemingwayApp.com and paste your homepage copy. Check if you're at grade 5-8.

  2. Count Your CTAs

    How many different actions can visitors take? If more than 1-2, you have a problem. Choose ONE primary action.

  3. Evaluate Your Call Out

    Does your headline clearly say WHO your product is for? If not, make it bigger and clearer.

  4. Check Button Consistency

    Do all your buttons use the same language? Fix any confusing button copy.

  5. Review Your Sales Flow

    Are you selling people on your free product? Or just giving them a download link?

Phase 2: Build Your AI Assistant (20 Minutes)

  1. Open ChatGPT

    Start with regular ChatGPT (not custom GPT yet)

  2. Create Your Research Prompt 
    I want you to give me a deep research prompt to create
a copywriting guide for this website to help improve copy.

[Paste your entire website copy here]

Ask me clarifying questions to understand my market better.
  3. Answer the Questions

    ChatGPT will interview you about your market, audience, offers, founder story, and brand voice. Be specific!

  4. Generate Your Guide

    Take the research prompt and run it through ChatGPT Deep Research mode. This will take about 20 minutes and produce a 60-70 page guide.

  5. Create Your Custom GPT

    Upload the research guide to a new custom GPT. Add any market research data you have. Now you have your personal copywriting assistant!

Phase 3: Optimize Your Copy (Ongoing)

Now use your custom GPT to improve any page:

Help me analyze this copy and improve it.

Ask me multiple choice questions to help me identify
and give you the best input to improve this copy.

[Paste your page copy]

The AI will ask clarifying questions about:

  • Primary goal of the page
  • Stage of awareness (problem aware, solution aware, product aware)
  • Emotional driver or transformation
  • Target audience
  • Page type (landing page, sales page, homepage)
