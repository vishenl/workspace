üéØ Key Strategic Recommendations

  • Build Daily Journaling First - Higher engagement (2.5x retention), no permission barriers, better recommendations
  • Integrate Within Mindvalley App - Zero acquisition cost, natural cross-sell to Quests, ecosystem lock-in
  • Target 12-15% 66-day Retention - 3-4x industry average (4% baseline)
  • Phase 2: Add HealthKit - Layer biometric data as premium feature after users engaged
  • Evaluate Spinout at Month 7 - If 20%+ adoption, consider standalone app for broader market
