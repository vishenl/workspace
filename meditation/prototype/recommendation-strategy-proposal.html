  • Simpler onboarding - "Daily journaling + meditation" easier to explain than Mindvalley ecosystem
  • Lower friction for new users - No Quest commitment required to start
  • Separate brand positioning - Can target meditation-only audience (larger TAM)

  • Competitive market - Headspace/Calm already dominate meditation space
