00:00-08:00 OPENING SEQUENCE

00:00-02:00

COLD OPEN

What Happens: Vishen opens with Kirlian photography of a leaf's aura, introduces the concept of energy body, connects to saints and holy men.

What's Working

  • Visual hook with Kirlian photography (strong pattern interrupt)
  • Authority building with European church paintings
  • Creates curiosity about universal capability

What Needs Improvement

  • Opens with "Welcome everyone" (weak - no hook, no immediate problem identification)
  • Too much passive voice ("What you're seeing here is...")
  • Missing the Big Domino Statement upfront
  • No clear promise of transformation in first 30 seconds
  • Lacks urgency/reason to pay attention NOW

Specific Edits:

"Welcome, everyone, to our masterclass on embracing your energy body. First, I want you to take a look at this picture."
"PAUSE THIS VIDEO if you're not ready to completely change how you experience reality‚Äî because in the next 80 minutes, you're going to discover why everything you've been taught about creating your life is backwards. [PAUSE: 2 seconds] Look at this image. [VISUAL: Kirlian photography appears] This is a photograph... of something you can't normally see with your naked eye."

DIRECTOR'S NOTES

  • [PACING]: Slow, deliberate opening. Make them stop scrolling
  • [DELIVERY]: Say "PAUSE THIS VIDEO" with authority, not as a suggestion
  • [VISUAL]: Don't show the image until AFTER the setup
  • [PAUSE]: Full 2-second silence before revealing image
