Do I need prior coaching experience to join?

No prior coaching experience is required. This certification is designed to take you from complete beginner to confident, skilled coach. We'll teach you everything from the spiritual foundations to the business systems.

What's the time commitment for the 2-month online portion?

Expect to invest 5-8 hours per week for the online training. This includes live group calls (recorded if you can't attend), self-paced modules, practice sessions with peers, and implementation work.

Is travel and accommodation included in the investment?

No, the $7,777 investment covers the full certification program (3-day Sevilla immersion + 2-month online training). Travel, accommodation, and meals are separate and arranged by you.
