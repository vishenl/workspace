The event is hosted at Pullman Miami Airport in Miami, Florida. We strongly encourage staying at the host hotel as the training days are long and intensive, with practice sessions continuing late into the evening.

Fly into Miami International Airport (MIA). The Pullman Miami Airport hotel is conveniently located near the airport.

Arrive by November 9, 2025 (evening before). Plan to depart November 15, 2025 (morning after). There is a special evening event on November 14 that all participants must attend.

Training hours vary daily. Expect long, intensive days with deep work. There will be limited free time for city exploration due to the immersive nature of the program.

Business casual attire for daily training sessions. Bring one special outfit for the final evening event on November 14. Also consider professional photo opportunities throughout the week.

Will I get time with Eric?

Yes! Eric facilitates the daily training personally. You'll have substantial direct interaction time with Eric throughout all five days, including one-on-one feedback sessions and small group work.

We discourage bringing companions. The intensive focus required for transformation works best without distractions. There's significant practice group work happening outside of formal sessions.
