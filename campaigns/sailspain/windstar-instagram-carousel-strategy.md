## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Navy blue (#0066CC) - Mindvalley brand
- **Accent:** Gold (#D4AF37) - Luxury, premium
- **Background:** White/Cream (#FFFFFF, #F8F9FA)
- **Text:** Charcoal (#1A1A1A)
- **Mediterranean:** Terracotta, ocean blues, warm whites

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Playfair Display (serif) - elegant, sophisticated
- **Body:** Inter (sans-serif) - clean, readable
- **Accents:** All caps for emphasis, generous letter spacing

### Image Treatment
- **Filters:** Warm, Mediterranean tones (slight orange/teal grade)
- **Overlay:** Dark gradient on text-heavy slides (60-70% opacity)
- **Consistency:** All images should feel cohesive (similar lighting, warmth, saturation)

### Instagram Specs
- **Size:** 1080 x 1080px (square)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **File size:** Under 8MB per slide
- **Total:** 10 slides maximum
