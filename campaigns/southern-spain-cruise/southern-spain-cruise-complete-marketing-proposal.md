## üé® Copywriting Framework Applied

### **Eugene Schwartz (Breakthrough Advertising)**
- **Awareness Stage Matching:** Targeting Stage 3-4 (solution-aware, product-aware)
- **Market Sophistication:** Stage 4-5 (transformation + identity positioning)
- **Unique Mechanism:** "Mediterranean Reset Protocol" (proprietary framework)
- **Mass Desire:** Tap into universal desire for clarity, purpose, transformation

### **Robert Cialdini (7 Principles of Influence)**
- **Reciprocity:** Free webinar, value-first emails
- **Commitment:** Micro-yeses throughout funnel
- **Social Proof:** 20M students, testimonials, sold-out messaging
- **Authority:** Vishen's credentials, NYT bestsellers, $100M company
- **Liking:** Vulnerable storytelling, shared values
- **Scarcity:** 312 guests, 25 Premium, 8 VIP (authentic capacity limits)
- **Unity:** "Your people," tribe language, community emphasis

### **Joe Sugarman (Slippery Slope)**
- **Subject lines:** Create curiosity gap that guarantees open
- **Opening hooks:** First sentence makes second irresistible
- **Smooth transitions:** No friction points, natural flow
- **Multiple CTAs:** At every natural decision moment
- **P.S. usage:** Reinforce key message, create FOMO

### **Mindvalley Voice Calibration**
- **Sophisticated tone:** No hype, no manipulation
- **Evidence-based:** Scientific backing for claims
- **Transparent:** Clear pricing, honest about experience
- **Honors intelligence:** Treats audience as smart, discerning
- **Multi-dimensional:** Transformation + luxury + culture
