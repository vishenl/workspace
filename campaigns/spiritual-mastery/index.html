What is the SMOI?

The Spiritual Mastery Outcomes Index (SMOI) is a validated 3-component assessment that measures genuine transformation across everyday well-being, mystical experiences, and stress reduction.

Administered at Week 0 (baseline), Week 6 (mid-course), and Week 22 (graduation) to track real, measurable spiritual growth.

WHO-5 Well-Being

0-100% Scale

Measures everyday flourishing: feeling cheerful, calm, active, rested, and interested in life over the past 2 weeks.

MEQ-30 Mystical Experience

0-100% Scale

Captures peak spiritual states: unity, sacred awareness, transcendence, profound truth, and transformative insights.

PSS-10 Stress Reduction

0-100% Scale (Inverted)

Measures perceived stress levels and self-efficacy: confidence in handling life's challenges over the past month.
