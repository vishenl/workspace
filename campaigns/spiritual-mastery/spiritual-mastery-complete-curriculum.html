Spiritual Mastery Outcomes Index (SMOI)

The SMOI is a validated 3-component assessment measuring transformation across everyday well-being, mystical experiences, and stress reduction. Administered at Week 0 (baseline), Week 6 (mid-course), and Week 22 (graduation).

The Three SMOI Components

1. WHO-5 Well-Being (0-100%)

Measures: Everyday flourishing over past 2 weeks

  • Felt cheerful and in good spirits
  • Felt calm and relaxed
  • Felt active and vigorous
  • Woke up feeling fresh and rested
  • Daily life filled with interesting things

Target: >50% (below indicates intervention needed)

2. MEQ-30 Mystical Experience (0-100%)

Measures: Depth/quality of spiritual/mystical states during practice

  • Mystical: Unity, sacred awareness, oneness, divine contact
  • Positive Mood: Joy, ecstasy, gratitude, awe, bliss
  • Transcendence: Timelessness, infinite vastness, beyond body
  • Noetic Quality: Profound truth, deep knowing, life transformation

Captures: Peak spiritual experiences (not daily practice)

3. PSS-10 Perceived Stress (0-100%, inverted)

Measures: Stress levels in past month (scored inversely - higher is better)

  • Stress Subscale: Upset, nervous, stressed, unable to control, difficulties piling up
  • Self-Efficacy Subscale: Confident, able to handle, things going your way, on top of things

Scoring: Inverted so higher = less stress = better (0-13 Low, 14-26 Moderate, 27-40 High stress)
