About Neale

Neale Donald Walsch is the author of 40 books on contemporary spirituality and its practical application in everyday life, including seven New York Times bestsellers. His Conversations with God series has been translated into 35 languages and read by millions around the world.

His most recent title is GODTALK: Experiences of Humanity's Connections with a Higher Power. Other titles include The God Solution, and God's Message to the World: You've got me all wrong. His books are said to have redefined the concept of God and shifted spiritual paradigms around the globe.

He offers powerful talks focused extensively on what he calls the most important question facing humanity today: Is it possible that there is something we don't fully understand about God and about Life, the understanding of which would change everything?
