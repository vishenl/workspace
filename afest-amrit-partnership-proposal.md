## Executive Summary

## Proposed Partnership Structure

### Event Concept: A-Fest Longevity Edition

**Theme:** "The Extraordinary Life Extension - Transforming Consciousness & Biology for a 100+ Year Life"

**Duration:** 5 days / 4 nights

**Target Dates:** Q1 or Q4 2026 (avoiding peak summer heat)

**Expected Attendance:** 400 participants

**Pricing Model:** Premium ticket pricing ($4,000-$7,000 per person) covering accommodations, meals, programming, spa access

### Integrated Programming Framework

**Morning (7:00 AM - 12:00 PM)**
- Sunrise meditation on the beach
- Yoga and embodiment practices
- Longevity optimization workshops (fasting protocols, sleep science, etc.)
- Access to Aayush hydrothermal circuit

**Afternoon (2:00 PM - 6:00 PM)**
- Keynote talks from longevity and transformation experts
- Breakout workshops (brain health, hormonal optimization, regenerative medicine)
- Personalized wellness assessments (optional add-on)
- Integration of Amrit's signature programming

**Evening (7:00 PM - 11:00 PM)**
- Wellness-focused gourmet dinners
- Celebration events and performances
- Starlight beach gatherings
- Sacred connection experiences

### Revenue Sharing Model (Suggested Framework)

**Option 1: Traditional Event Partnership**
- A-Fest pays negotiated rate for venue, accommodations, F&B, spa access
- Amrit provides discounted group rate reflecting partnership value
- A-Fest handles ticketing, keeps ticket revenue
- Amrit keeps all ancillary spa/treatment revenue
- Joint marketing responsibilities

**Option 2: Co-Created Experience**
- Revenue split on ticket sales (e.g., 60/40 or 70/30 to A-Fest)
- A-Fest provides speakers, content creation, community, brand
- Amrit provides venue, operations, wellness programming, staff
- Shared marketing investment with agreed split
- Joint ownership of event IP for future editions

**Option 3: Hybrid Model**
- Base facility fee to Amrit guarantees coverage of costs
- Revenue share on ticket sales above threshold
- Shared upside incentivizes both parties to maximize attendance
- Ancillary services (additional spa treatments, private consultations) retained by Amrit
- Loyalty/membership incentives: Attendees receive Amrit membership discounts

### Marketing & Promotion

**Joint Responsibilities:**
- Co-branded event website and materials
- Shared social media promotion across both platforms
- Joint press releases to wellness and business media
- Email campaigns to both databases (1M+ Mindvalley subscribers + Amrit's wellness network)

**A-Fest/Mindvalley Leads:**
- Event production and speaker curation
- Attendee experience design
- Community engagement and pre-event connection
- Content creation (videos, podcasts promoting partnership)

**Amrit Leads:**
- Wellness programming integration
- Spa and treatment offerings
- Culinary experience design
- Local partnerships (transportation, activities)

### Success Metrics

**For A-Fest:**
- Sell out 400 tickets within 3 months of announcement
- 9.0+ average attendee satisfaction score
- 30%+ attendees interested in returning to Amrit
- 50+ pieces of content generated (social, testimonials)

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Challenges & Solutions

| Challenge | Mitigation Strategy |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Weather/Season** | Schedule for optimal Florida weather (Feb-April or Oct-Nov); backup indoor spaces for all activities |
| **Capacity Constraints** | Cap at 400 attendees matching A-Fest's typical size and Amrit's capacity |
| **Brand Alignment** | Co-create detailed brand guidelines; regular alignment meetings; approval processes for all joint materials |
| **Financial Risk** | Phased payment structure; cancellation insurance; minimum guarantee with upside share |
| **Operational Complexity** | Dedicated partnership manager from each organization; weekly planning calls; clear decision-making framework |
| **Market Reception** | Early bird pricing to test demand; leverage Mindvalley's existing A-Fest community; pre-event surveying |
