# A-Fest x Amrit Ocean Resort Founder Meeting Agenda

**Date:** TBD
**Time:** 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM (2 hours)
**Location:** TBD (Virtual or In-Person)

**Attendees:**
- Vishen Lakhiani (Founder, Mindvalley / A-Fest)
- Dilip Barot (Founder, Amrit Ocean Resort)
- Neera Barot (Co-Founder, Amrit Ocean Resort)
- Key team members from both organizations (optional)

---

## Meeting Objectives

1. Establish personal connection and values alignment between founders
2. Gain clarity on partnership vision, structure, and logistics
3. Identify potential challenges and solutions
4. Determine next steps and timeline for decision-making

---

## Agenda

### 10:00 - 10:15 | Welcome & Personal Introductions (15 min)

**Purpose:** Build rapport and understand each founder's personal journey

**Discussion Points:**
- Personal stories: What led each founder to create their organization?
- Core values and what drives each founder's work
- Vision for impact: What does success look like in 5-10 years?

---

### 10:15 - 10:30 | Partnership Vision Alignment (15 min)

**Purpose:** Ensure both parties see the partnership's potential similarly

**Key Questions to Discuss:**

1. **Shared Vision**
   - Does the longevity-focused A-Fest concept resonate with both founders?
   - How does this partnership advance each organization's core mission?
   - What excites you most about this potential collaboration?

2. **Values Alignment**
   - How do you see our organizational values aligning (or potentially conflicting)?
   - What non-negotiable values must be honored in this partnership?
   - How do we ensure authenticity vs. commercialization of wellness/transformation?

3. **Long-Term Potential**
   - Is this a one-time event or the foundation for ongoing collaboration?
   - What does success look like beyond the first event?
   - Could this evolve into deeper content/program partnerships?

---

### 10:30 - 11:00 | Partnership Structure & Economics (30 min)

**Purpose:** Gain clarity on business model, responsibilities, and financial arrangements

**Key Questions to Discuss:**

1. **Event Scale & Capacity**
   - Is 400 attendees the right target, or should we consider different scale options?
   - What's Amrit's realistic capacity during peak vs. off-peak seasons?
   - Should we plan for single event or multiple cohorts/year?

2. **Revenue Model Clarity**
   - Which revenue-sharing model feels most fair and motivating for both parties?
   - What are the actual costs for Amrit (rooms, F&B, spa, staffing, operations)?
   - What ticket pricing makes sense given value delivered and market positioning?
   - How do we handle ancillary revenue (spa upgrades, extensions, private sessions)?

3. **Investment & Risk Sharing**
   - What upfront investments are needed from each party?
   - How do we share financial risk (deposits, cancellation insurance, guarantees)?
   - What happens if we don't hit attendance targets?
   - Marketing budget: Who invests what in promotion?

4. **Operational Responsibilities**
   - What does A-Fest own (content, speakers, community, production)?
   - What does Amrit own (venue, F&B, spa operations, local logistics)?
   - Who manages attendee experience, communications, registration?
   - How do we handle co-branding, joint marketing, PR?

5. **Contract & Legal Considerations**
   - Exclusivity: Can A-Fest work with other wellness resorts? Can Amrit host competing events?
   - IP ownership: Who owns event content, recordings, attendee data?
   - Liability and insurance requirements?
   - Force majeure and cancellation terms?

---

### 11:00 - 11:25 | Event Experience Design (25 min)

**Purpose:** Co-create the attendee experience and ensure operational alignment

**Key Questions to Discuss:**

1. **Programming Integration**
   - How do we blend A-Fest's transformation programming with Amrit's wellness offerings?
   - Which Amrit team members/practitioners participate in programming?
   - Can we co-create signature experiences unique to this partnership?
   - How much structure vs. free time is optimal?

2. **Attendee Experience**
   - What's included in base ticket (spa access, treatments, activities)?
   - How do we tier offerings (standard, VIP, ultra-premium)?
   - What optional add-ons create additional value and revenue?
   - How do we personalize experiences (wellness assessments, custom protocols)?

3. **Culinary Vision**
   - How do A-Fest's dining expectations align with Amrit's wellness culinary philosophy?
   - Alcohol policy: How do we handle this given transformation + longevity focus?
   - Special dietary needs and customization capabilities?
   - Evening celebration vs. wellness dining balance?

4. **Timeline & Seasonality**
   - What are optimal dates considering Florida weather, Amrit's occupancy, A-Fest community availability?
   - How much lead time does Amrit need for preparation?
   - What's the decision deadline to lock in dates for 2026?

5. **Logistics & Operations**
   - Transportation: Airport transfers, local activities?
   - Accommodation distribution: How do we assign rooms/suites fairly?
   - Technology needs: AV, production, wifi, app integration?
   - Staffing: Ratios, training, partnership coordination?

---

### 11:25 - 11:45 | Challenges, Concerns & Solutions (20 min)

**Purpose:** Surface potential obstacles and problem-solve proactively

**Key Questions to Discuss:**

1. **Capacity & Constraints**
   - What operational constraints might limit the partnership's success?
   - Are there any facilities/amenities not available that A-Fest typically requires?
   - What happens if demand significantly exceeds or falls short of 400 attendees?

2. **Brand Considerations**
   - How do we protect both brands while co-creating something new?
   - What approval processes are needed for marketing materials, messaging, visuals?
   - How do we handle potential negative feedback or issues?

3. **Competitive Landscape**
   - Does either organization have existing partnerships that could conflict?
   - How do we position this vs. other wellness retreats/transformation events?
   - What makes this partnership defensible and unique in market?

4. **Cultural Fit**
   - Do our teams' working styles and cultures align?
   - How do we handle decision-making when we disagree?
   - What communication cadence and protocols work best?

5. **Measurement & Accountability**
   - What metrics define success beyond financial returns?
   - How do we measure attendee satisfaction, transformation outcomes, health impacts?
   - What feedback loops ensure continuous improvement?
   - How do we evaluate whether to continue partnership after first event?

---

### 11:45 - 12:00 | Next Steps & Decision Timeline (15 min)

**Purpose:** Establish clear action items and decision-making process

**Key Questions to Discuss:**

1. **Immediate Next Steps**
   - What additional information does each party need before committing?
   - Should we schedule a site visit for Vishen and team to experience Amrit firsthand?
   - Who are the key stakeholders that need to be involved in final decision?
   - What's the timeline for due diligence, contract negotiation, announcement?

2. **Decision-Making Framework**
   - What are the go/no-go criteria for each organization?
   - What's the deadline for making a decision on partnership?
   - If we move forward, what are the key milestones leading to launch?
   - How do we stay aligned during planning phase?

3. **Communication Protocol**
   - Who are the primary points of contact from each organization?
   - How frequently should we meet during exploration phase?
   - When/how do we involve broader teams in planning?

4. **Public Positioning**
   - When would we announce partnership publicly?
   - How do we manage confidentiality until official announcement?
   - What's the PR and marketing rollout strategy?

## Meeting Ground Rules

1. **Openness:** Share concerns and constraints honestly
2. **Curiosity:** Seek to understand before being understood
3. **Possibility:** Explore creative solutions to challenges
4. **Respect:** Honor each organization's values and boundaries
5. **Clarity:** Leave with clear next steps and responsibilities

---

## Post-Meeting Deliverables

Within 48 hours of meeting:

1. **Meeting Summary:** Shared notes capturing key insights, decisions, and open questions
2. **Action Items:** Clear list of who does what by when
3. **Decision Timeline:** Agreed-upon dates for next steps and final decision
4. **Follow-Up Schedule:** Calendar invites for subsequent meetings/site visit

---

## Success Indicators for This Meeting

This meeting is successful if:

✅ Both founders feel genuine excitement about partnership potential
✅ Major concerns or dealbreakers are surfaced and discussed
✅ There's clarity on next steps and decision-making process
✅ Financial and operational feasibility is better understood
✅ Personal connection and trust is established between founders
✅ Timeline for moving forward (or not) is mutually agreed upon
