Critical Insights

7 Key Trends Requiring Attention

Based on comprehensive analysis across all lessons and teachers, these patterns emerged as requiring immediate action.

CRITICAL

Pace Too Fast

Issue: Students consistently report content delivery is too rapid to absorb. Particularly affects Vishen and Daniel's sessions. Students feeling overwhelmed and missing key concepts.

Fix: Reduce content volume by 30-40%, build in "pause points" every 15 minutes, allow time for note-taking.

CRITICAL

Resource Accessibility Issues

Issue: Materials not shared in time, chat/Q&A not accessible in replays, downloadable resources have errors, AI prompts missing from resources.

Fix: Share ALL materials BEFORE sessions, make chat available in replays, create downloadable resource packs, quality check all PDFs.

HIGH

Beginner vs. Advanced Gap

Issue: Content assumes higher level of knowledge than many possess. Students expected support for "starting from scratch" but curriculum assumes existing business, AI literacy, and team.

Fix: Create "foundations" module for beginners, provide "no business yet" pathway, offer pre-work assignments to level-set.

HIGH

Platform Frustrations

Issue: Webinar-style format prevents peer interaction. Students want to see who's attending, connect with other entrepreneurs, and participate interactively like Ajit's Zoom sessions.

Fix: Switch to interactive Zoom format across ALL sessions, enable participant visibility, create breakout exercises.

HIGH

Need More "How-To" Steps

Issue: Students appreciate frameworks but want more tactical implementation guidance. They need templates, step-by-step guides, clear "next steps" after each lesson.

Fix: Include "Implementation Bridge" - every concept needs What + Why + HOW with downloadable templates.

MEDIUM

Pod Structure Requested

Issue: Students want structured peer collaboration, not just access to content. Request for official pods to work together and build GPTs collaboratively.

Fix: Assign students to small accountability pods (5-7 people), provide pod exercises, enable long-term relationships.

HIGH

Undisclosed Costs

Issue: Students didn't realize additional investments required (ChatGPT Plus/Pro). Feeling surprised by costs not disclosed upfront creates frustration and distrust.

Fix: Create "Tech Stack You'll Need" document before enrollment listing ALL tools and costs. No surprise expenses.
