Risk Mitigation

Key Risks & Mitigation Strategies

1. Market Acceptance Risk

Risk: LATAM audience may not adopt subscription model

Mitigation:

  • Beta test with EXMA community before full launch
  • Offer 3-month free trial to EXMA attendees
  • Start with most popular programs (manifestation, business, health)
  • LATAM-specific pricing ($29/mo vs $33.25)

2. Currency/Payment Risk

Risk: Currency volatility and payment processing challenges

Mitigation:

  • Accept multiple payment methods (cards, PayPal, OXXO, local transfers)
  • Offer annual plans for price stability
  • Price in USD but allow local currency payment
  • Partner with Stripe for LATAM payment optimization

3. Partnership Alignment Risk

Risk: Strategic misalignment between partners

Mitigation:

  • Clear governance structure with defined decision rights
  • Quarterly strategic reviews
  • Exit provisions in partnership agreement
  • 6-month pilot period with option to extend/terminate

4. Execution Risk

Risk: Inability to deliver on ambitious growth targets

Mitigation:

  • Hire experienced LATAM tech/education leader as CEO
  • Phase approach with clear milestones
  • Monthly KPI tracking and adjustment
  • Start with Mexico, expand only after proving model
